Game Instructions

  1. Use your pumpkin bucket to collect candies and earn points.
  2. If a candy falls to the ground, your candy streak resets to zero.
  3. Be cautious, not only candies drop; you might get a strike.
  4. Three strikes and the game's over.

Movement

Use "A" and "D" or "LEFT" and "RIGHT" arrow keys to move your pumpkin bucket.

Pause/Resume

While playing use "SPACE" or "ESC" keys to pause and resume the game.
