Game Instructions

Join the Halloween fun and embark on a candy-collecting adventure with your trusty pumpkin bucket! Your goal is to amass points by snatching up all the delicious candies that rain down from the spooky skies. But beware, if a single candy hits the ground, your hard-earned candy collection streak will plummet to zero.

And that's not all! In the eerie night, not only candies might tumble down; you could also be struck by an unexpected twist of fate. Accumulate three strikes, and your thrilling Halloween quest will come to a spine-tingling end. Stay vigilant and make this Halloween an epic candy-grabbing spectacle!

Movement

Use "A" and "D" or "LEFT" and "RIGHT" arrow keys to move your pumpkin bucket.

Pause/Resume

While playing use "SPACE" or "ESC" keys to pause and resume the game.
